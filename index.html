A little about me...

Sony Maharjan is trained as a classical realist. She has practiced the technique for more than 10 years. Currently she is pursuing her undergraduate degree in California College of the Arts (CCA) and exploring different forms of visual arts. Her training and practice from classical realism gave an invaluable foundation in visual arts and the education she is receiving at CCA is helping her to be a well-rounded artist. She believes in art as a powerful tool in self expression and a catalyst for a social change.
