A little about me...

I am trained as a classical realist and have practiced the technique for more than 10 years. Currently I am pursuing my undergraduate degree in California College of the Arts (CCA) and exploring different forms of visual arts. My training and practice from classical realism gave an invaluable foundation in visual arts and the education I am receiving at CCA is helping me to be a well-rounded artist. I believe in art as a powerful tool in self expression and a catalyst for a social change.
