Kathmandu Valley

Memoir from 2015 Earthquake

The Kathmandu Valley is still a home for me. All my relatives reside there and the memories from my childhood is still linked to that place. The Kathmandu Valley has lot of alleys. The alley where I grew up was very similar to this-- street dogs, burning trash, elder people relaxing at the patio are the usual scenes we still get to see today. All these images were captured right after the Earthquake of Nepal in 2015.

Mall Pokhari

Mall Pokhari is a government land that sits behind one of my family houses. This scene was captured on April 25, 2015 few hours after the massive earthquake that killed more than 9000 people. There were not a massive destruction around our area but the imapact was so big we were too scared to go back to our houses. We were living like this in the open field for about couple weeks.

Outskirts of Kathmandu Valley

The villages located at outskirts of Kathmandu valley are how they looked at my home town when I was growing up. The capital used to look similar to this. So much have changed because of growing urbanization.
