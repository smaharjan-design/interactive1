Kathmandu Valley

Memoir from 2015 Earthquake

The Kathmandu Valley is still a home for me. All my relatives reside there and my memories from my childhood is still linked to that place. The Kathmandu Valley has lot of alleys. The alley where I grew up was very similar to this-- street dogs, burning trash, elder people relaxing at the patio are the usual scenes we still get to see today. All these images were captured right after the Earthquake of Nepal in 2015.

Mall Pokhari

Mall Pokhari is a government land that sits behind one of my family houses. This scene is from April 25 of 2015 when we had a massive earthquake.
